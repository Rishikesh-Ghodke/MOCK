PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year Engineering (FE)

Engineering Mathematics I

Covers linear algebra, calculus, and differential equations.

Engineering Physics

Basics of optics, quantum mechanics, and electromagnetism.

Basic Electrical Engineering

Introduces concepts of circuits, power systems, and machines.

Engineering Graphics

Covers orthographic projections, isometric views, and CAD tools.

Second Year Engineering (SE)

Data Structures

Study of arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, and their algorithms.

Computer Organization

Covers CPU architecture, memory hierarchy, and instruction sets.

Discrete Mathematics

Includes logic, set theory, relations, functions, and combinatorics.

Object Oriented Programming

C++ and Java concepts like classes, inheritance, and polymorphism.

Third Year Engineering (TE)

Database Management Systems

Covers SQL, relational models, transactions, and normalization.

Operating Systems
