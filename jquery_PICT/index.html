Welcome to PICT

Established in 1983, PICT is a premier institute in Pune, Maharashtra, renowned for its academic excellence, vibrant campus life, and cutting-edge research in engineering and technology.

Why Choose PICT?

  • Highly qualified faculty and modern infrastructure
  • Strong industry connections and placement records
  • Active student clubs and technical events
  • Focus on innovation, research, and entrepreneurship

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a globally recognized institution.
Mission: To provide quality education and foster research and innovation.
