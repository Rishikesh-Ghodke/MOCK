Movie Reviews

Inception

Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller

Summary: A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea.

Review: A mind-bending thriller that challenges perception and delivers a powerful cinematic experience. Christopher Nolan at his best.

Parasite

Genre: Drama, Thriller

Summary: Greed and class discrimination threaten the newly formed symbiotic relationship between the wealthy and the destitute Kim family.

Review: A dark and clever social commentary packed with tension, humor, and brilliance. A deserving Oscar winner.

The Dark Knight

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Summary: Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice.

Review: An iconic performance by Heath Ledger and a gripping story make this one of the best superhero films ever made.

Interstellar

Genre: Adventure, Drama, Sci-Fi

Summary: A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival.

Review: Visually stunning and emotionally profound, Interstellar is a journey of science, love, and human endurance.
